Who Are We?

Located in Lake Wales, Florida, Ellison RBM, Inc. is your trusted partner in construction and maintenance of secondary roads, driveways, and parking pads. With our extensive experience and commitment to quality, we've established ourselves as a leading provider of construction and landscape supply services in Central Florida.

We take pride in offering comprehensive solutions, from our diverse range of landscape supplies including aggregates, mulch, fill, sand, shell rock, and river rock, to our professional concrete division and expert land clearing services. Whether you need materials delivered or picked up, our team ensures reliable service and superior quality for every project.
